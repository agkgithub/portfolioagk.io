MOVIE COLLECTION DASHBOARD

The main outcome of this dashboard is to visualize the overall revenue generated from the KGF franchise KGF 1 and KGF 2 by the production company under different parameters. For this, the revenue is sliced with different parameters viz. Collection based on region, Based on Weekday, Week number, comparison of the budget invested to the revenue generated etc.

SALES DATA DASHBOARD

The outcome of this data visualization is to visualize the sales and the profit generated by an online store with respect to different regions worldwide. The store sells products of different categories to the customers under different segments, for that, the sales data is visualized under different time segments - day, month, year, and proportion of the sales of different product categories to different customer segments etc.

EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS USING PYTHON

In this EDA activity, the revenue data of a ride-sharing company is considered and the relationship between different parameters is analysed and performed graphical visualization of different variables using Python programming.
